Diwali 2023 Date Calendar: Diwali, a joyous festival eagerly anticipated by almost everyone in India, is celebrated on the 15th day of the Hindu luni-solar month of Kartik. This year, the festival falls on November 12, making it a day of great significance. Often referred to as the “Festival of Lights,” Diwali is intrinsically associated with themes of happiness, wealth, prosperity, and warmth.

We have often heard from our elders to wear something new for Diwali and thus there is a tradition to purchase new clothes during Diwali. However, not everyone can afford new clothes every Diwali and thus they have to wear old clothes. It’s okay to wear old clothes but make sure it is not torn anywhere.
